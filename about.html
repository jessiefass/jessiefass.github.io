Welcome, my name is Jessie Fass. I am a middle school art teacher at two public middle schools in New York City (MS 933- City Knoll and MS177 Yorkville East) In addition to teaching art, I am also a practicing artist focusing on oil painting, and drawing. I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts from the Penny W. Stamps School of Art and Design at the University of Michigan, and then received my Master of Art: Teaching Art K-12 at New York University. Enjoy both my teaching portfolio as well as my personal art portfolio.
